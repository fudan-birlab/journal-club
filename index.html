Brain, Intelligence, and Robotics Journal Club

Brain, Intelligence, Robotics Journal Club meets periodically to discuss current studies and research in neuroscience, AI, and robotics.
The club primarily focuses on summarizing research literature at the start of a project, presenting results prepared for submission, and highlighting important studies and breakthroughs in the field.
